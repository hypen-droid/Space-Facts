What is a Galaxy?

A galaxy is a huge collection of gas, dust, and billions (a thousand millions '1,000,000,000') of stars and their solar systems. A galaxy is held together by gravity. Our galaxy, the Milky Way, also has a supermassive black hole (A supermassive black hole is a hole that pull everything) in the middle.
